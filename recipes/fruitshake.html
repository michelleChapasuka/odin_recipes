Fruitshake

Discription

Banana smoothies are a delicious and refreshing drink that combines the natural sweetness of bananas with a creamy texture.
They are perfect for a quick breakfast or a healthy snack,providing a tasty way to cool down on a hot day.
Easy to make and loved by many, banana shakes are a simple yet delightful treat that brings a smile to anyone's face.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

    • Instructions

    1. Add all the ingredients to a blender
    2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
      If the smoothie is too thick,add mor milk to reach your desired consistency
    3. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed
    4. Pour into a glass, sprinkle cinnamon for garnish

    ENJOY!!
